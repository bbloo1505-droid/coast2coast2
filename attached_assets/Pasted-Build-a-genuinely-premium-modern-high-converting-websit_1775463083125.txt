Build a genuinely premium, modern, high-converting website for a local business or contractor.

The website must feel custom, memorable, polished, and trustworthy. It should look like a business that is established, capable, easy to work with, and worth contacting.

This site is designed to maximise enquiries and booked work. Do not make it feel generic, overdesigned, or full of fluffy marketing language. Keep it simple, genuine, concise, straight to the point, and visually impressive.

Top priorities:
- Exceptional on mobile first, then desktop
- Fast, clean, responsive, and smooth
- Minimal words, strong structure, clear calls to action
- Premium design without looking corporate or fake
- Confident, modern, slightly bold
- Every section should have a purpose
- No clutter
- No filler cards
- No obvious AI-generated layout patterns
- No giant walls of text
- No excessive icons everywhere
- No cheesy stock-site look

Visual direction:
- Clean luxury-inspired service business aesthetic
- Strong typography
- Excellent spacing and rhythm
- Big, confident headings
- Concise supporting text
- Full-width sections
- Large, high-quality imagery
- Minimal but powerful use of colour
- Soft gradients, subtle shadows, refined borders
- Elegant contrast between text and background
- Modern, crisp, premium UI
- The design should feel expensive, even if the business is small

Animation direction:
- Use beautiful, smooth, tasteful animations that feel high-end
- Prioritise animation quality over animation quantity
- Subtle scroll reveals
- Smooth fade/slide/scale entrances
- Premium hover states
- Gentle parallax or layered movement where appropriate
- Smooth sticky header behaviour
- Refined button interactions
- Image reveals or project transitions
- Animated counters only if relevant
- Animations must be smooth on mobile and not hurt performance
- Do not overdo motion or make the site feel distracting

Mobile requirements:
- Design mobile first
- It must look incredible on a phone
- Clear hierarchy and spacing on small screens
- Thumb-friendly buttons
- Sticky mobile call-to-action
- Simple mobile navigation
- Strong above-the-fold section on mobile
- Content stacked cleanly with no cramped layouts
- Text must be easy to scan quickly
- Mobile should feel as premium as desktop, not like a cut-down version

Desktop requirements:
- Desktop should feel expansive, premium, and immersive
- Use strong layout composition and spacing
- Hero section should feel impressive and intentional
- Create a polished, high-end feel without unnecessary complexity

Site goals:
- Make the business look trustworthy
- Make the business stand out from competitors
- Show what they do quickly
- Show why someone should choose them
- Make it effortless to contact them
- Turn visitors into enquiries

Tone:
- Genuine
- Clear
- Direct
- Confident
- Not pushy
- Not corporate
- Not overly “salesy”
- Not full of hype words
- Write like a real good business owner, not a marketing robot

Structure:
Create a clear, conversion-focused structure such as:
1. Hero section
2. Services overview
3. Why choose us / difference
4. Selected projects, work examples, or results
5. Simple process
6. Testimonials or trust signals
7. Strong call to action
8. Contact section / quote form

Hero section requirements:
- Strong headline that clearly says what the business does
- Short supporting text only
- Primary call-to-action button
- Secondary call-to-action if useful
- Strong visual impact immediately
- The hero should look world-class on both desktop and mobile
- Avoid long paragraphs

Content rules:
- Keep copy concise
- Every sentence must earn its place
- Avoid generic filler like “we are committed to excellence”
- Avoid fake-sounding marketing language
- Use clear, grounded wording
- Prioritise clarity over cleverness
- Use short sections that are easy to scan
- The site should feel premium because of design, structure, and confidence, not because of too much text

UI rules:
- Use fewer, better sections
- Avoid repetitive card grids unless truly needed
- If cards are used, make them feel intentional, minimal, and custom
- Use varied layout composition so the site does not feel templated
- Mix text, imagery, spacing, and section structure in a visually refined way
- Buttons should feel premium and modern
- Forms should be simple and easy to complete
- Navigation should be clean and minimal
- Add trust elements naturally without clutter

Conversion rules:
- Calls to action should appear in the right places without feeling spammy
- Make phone, email, quote request, or booking actions obvious
- Build trust quickly with visuals, wording, and proof
- Make the next step feel easy and low-friction

Technical expectations:
- Fully responsive
- Fast-loading
- SEO-friendly structure
- Accessible contrast and readable typography
- Clean semantic HTML structure
- Smooth performance on mobile
- Production-quality design and code

Important:
This should feel like one of the best small business websites someone has ever seen.
It must be simple, sharp, memorable, and believable.
Do not make it look like a generic AI-generated landing page.
Do not over-explain.
Do not over-design.
Make it feel premium, human, and highly effective.
